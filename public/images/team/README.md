# Team Photos

Place team member portrait photos here. The site expects the following filenames:

| File | Team Member |
|---|---|
| `team-1.jpg` | Arjun Mehta — Founder & CEO |
| `team-2.jpg` | Priya Nair — Head of ESG Strategy |
| `team-3.jpg` | Ravi Krishnaswamy — Chief Technology Officer |
| `team-4.jpg` | Kavya Reddy — Head of Capability Building |

**Photo specs:**
- Format: JPG or WebP
- Aspect ratio: 3:4 portrait (e.g. 600×800px)
- Minimum: 400×530px
- The photo will be displayed as a cover image; frame faces in the upper-centre third

If a photo file is missing or fails to load, the card automatically falls back to a gradient background with initials — so the site always looks good regardless of whether photos are present.
